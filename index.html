About Me

I am a Ph.D candidate at the University of Gronigen, under the supervisor of Prof.Lambert Schomaker and Prof.Hamidreza Kasaei . My research interest broadly lies in machine learning, intelligent robotics, automated processes, especially the integration of data-driven learning and planning in robotics applications. In recent times, I found myself getting more energized by applying ML in impactble problems. So, I am excited to explore new research directions, such as recommendation systems, RL in multi-objective optimization, vision-guided RL.
