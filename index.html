About Me

I am currently a researcher in the field of AI and Robotics. I received my Ph.D. in March 2024 under the supervision of Prof.Lambert Schomaker and Dr.Hamidreza Kasaei from the University of Groningen, The Netherlands. After a brief period in industry, I rekindled my passion for research. My research interests broadly lie in machine learning and autonomous robotics, especially in the combination of data-driven learning methods and planning in robotics motion planning applications.
